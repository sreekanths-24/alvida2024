"Alvida 2k24" is a farewell event for our seniors of Providence Girls' HSS. This farewell is a heartfelt moment to express our appreciation for our seniors and a time to celebrate the growth, laughter, and challenges that have shaped us. It's time to say goodbye and good luck to our seniors as they embark on their next journey.

VENUE

Providence Junior School Auditorium.

When?

February 9th, 2024 at 11 AM.
